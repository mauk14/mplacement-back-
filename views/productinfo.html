Whoopie Pie Cake

Ingredients:

Cake: Unsalted butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar, large egg, pure vanilla extract, cup all-purpose flour, cup unsweetened cocoa, baking soda, salt, cups milk
Filling: Unsalted butter, cups powdered sugar sifted, marshmallow fluff, vanilla extract, tsp salt, milk or heavy cream
